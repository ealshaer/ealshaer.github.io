Students' Testimony

I have been always blessed by working with smart, dedicated and hardworking students for the past years of my career. My research group has been very productive producing innovative research publishing many papers in high tier conferences and reputable venues. I am proud of their accomplishment during the PhD and during their career afterwards. Many of them got good satisfying jobs months in academia and industry many months before their dissertation defense.

I took the PhD advising/supervising role very seriously; and known of my dedication and support to my students. We always maintain professional and caring relationship that goes beyond the research and publication business. As a result, I put a significant effort in guiding and mentoring my PhD students, which always reflected very well on the students performance and success. My goal is to train my students to be highly innovative, strongly competitive, and caring to contribute to society.

For all my students, and particularly those who expressed their warm feelings and support, I will always value your genuine effort, appreciate the great time we spent together, and always looking forward to supporting you in your forthcoming professional and personal life. So please keep in touch.
